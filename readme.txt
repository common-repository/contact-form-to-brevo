Add Contact Form 7 Data to Brevo Contact lists.

== Description ==

Contact Form 7 is the most popular WordPress contact form builder and Contact Form to Brevo developed as addon of Contact Form 7, helps to extend your subscription lists and collect unlimited leads .You can easily add your contacts in Brevo when your contact form is submitted.



=== Features === 

Adds subscription in "Brevo" through "Contact Form 7"
Option to select "Brevo" list ID for subscribers
Option to select "Contact Form 7" fields for "Brevo" list
Constant updates

== Installation ==
1. Unzip contactform-to-brevo.zip
2. Upload all the files to the /wp-content/plugins/contactfrom-to-brevo
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
4. For customizing the plugin's settings, open "Contact Form 7"  form and go to "Brevo" tab
